Patient Guidance

What are the clinic timings?

Appointments can be taken starting from 11:00 AM. Dr. Shravan Alva is available at the clinic for consultations from 05:00 PM to 07:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. The clinic is closed on Sundays.

How do I reach the clinic?

We are located on the 1st Floor of Kumudavathi Building in Balmatta, Mangaluru.

Do you perform surgeries?

Yes, Dr. Shravan Alva performs various advanced procedures including:
• Conventional and Endoscopic Septoplasty
• Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS)
• Microscopic and Endoscopic Ear Surgeries
• Voice Disorder Surgeries
• Head and Neck Surgeries (including Thyroid and Cancer surgeries)
